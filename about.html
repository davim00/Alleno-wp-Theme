Overview

As a versatile and enthusiastic graphic artist for over 15 years, I have extensive experience working with large and small businesses and non-profit organizations with branding, print design and web technologies. I have a passion for great design and a desire to see it work toward the success of my clients. In this modern culture, image and engagement are key, and in that respect I strive to bring quality work utilizing new technologies, while building upon my past experience to create ideas. I approach every project focused to build client relationships with a positive and approachable attitude.

Projects

Below is a list of the common projects which I have experience working on:

  • Print design: business stationery, brochures, marketing material, newsletters, catalogs, menus
  • Branding: logo design, signage, retail/trade displays, corporate brand packages, social media graphics
  • Web design: custom websites, mobile conversions, Wordpress/Joomla/DotNetNuke themes
  • Digital graphics: icons, illustrations
  • Photography: photo retouch, restoration, effects, e-commerce
  • Products and packaging: product design, die-line creation, package design

Location

I work part-time as a freelance designer from my home office in the beautiful community of Arcadia, NC (about 20 minutes north of the center of Lexington), and a convenient 30 minute drive from High Point, Greensboro and Winston-Salem.
